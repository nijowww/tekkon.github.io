<p><b>Welcome to TEKKON!</b></p>

<p><span>TEKKON is a Web3 app that promotes social good by empowering users to help fix their local community&rsquo;s infrastructure.</span></p>

<p><span>Players earn tokens for taking photos and reviewing infrastructure.</span></p>
